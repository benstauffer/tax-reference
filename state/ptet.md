# PTET (Pass-Through Entity Tax) Details (2026)

Workaround for SALT cap. Entity pays state tax, owners get credit/exclusion.

SALT Cap: 40,400 (increased from 10K via OBBBA). Expires December 31, 2029.

## State Notes

- **CA:** 5-yr carryforward, expires 2030
- **CT:** Now ELECTIVE since 2024
- **GA:** Rate reduced per HB 111
- **HI:** Uses individual rates (Act 50, 2023)
- **ID:** Rate reduced per HB 40
- **IL:** PERMANENT (sunset removed Dec 2025)
- **IN:** Rate tied to IN AGIT rate
- **KY:** Rate reduced per HB 1
- **MA:** 5% rate (not 9% surtax rate)
- **MD:** Rate is 5.75% + local county rate
- **MO:** 4.7% flat per HB 798
- **MT:** Rate reduced per HB 337
- **NE:** Rate reduced per LB754
- **NJ:** Top rate 10.9% (not 10.75%)
- **NY:** Election by March 15. NYC has separate PTET.
- **OH:** CAT only (exclusion basis)
- **OK:** Top rate reduced for 2026
- **SC:** Top rate reduced for 2026
- **UT:** Verify availability — tied to SALT cap existence

## Expired / Pending

- **MN:** EXPIRED 12/31/2025 — NOT available for 2026
- **VA:** Pending extension to Jan 1, 2027 — verify status

## States Without PTET

AK, DE, FL, NV, NH, ND, PA, SD, TN, TX, WA, WY

## Election Timing

Most states: before tax year or with extension. California: original return due date. New York: March 15 for calendar year.
