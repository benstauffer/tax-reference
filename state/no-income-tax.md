# No Income Tax States (2026)

## Alaska
No state income tax. No capital gains tax.

---

## Florida
No state income tax. No capital gains tax.

---

## Nevada
No state income tax. No capital gains tax.

Commerce Tax: 0.051%-0.331% on gross receipts over 4M (rate varies by industry).

---

## New Hampshire
**Source:** [NH DRA](https://www.revenue.nh.gov/)

No state income tax on wages. No capital gains tax. Interest & dividends tax REPEALED effective Jan 1, 2025.

Business Profits Tax: 7.5% (treats S-Corps same as C-Corps).

---

## South Dakota
No state income tax. No capital gains tax.

---

## Tennessee
No state income tax on wages. No capital gains tax. Hall Tax on interest/dividends fully repealed 2021.

Franchise & Excise Tax: 6.5% + 0.25% on net worth (treats S-Corps same as C-Corps, min 100).

---

## Texas
No state income tax. No capital gains tax.

Margin Tax (Franchise Tax): Retail/Wholesale 0.375%, Other 0.75%, EZ Computation 0.331%, No Tax Threshold 2,650,000. Most small businesses pay nothing.

---

## Washington
**Source:** [WA DOR](https://dor.wa.gov/taxes-rates/other-taxes/capital-gains-tax)

No state income tax on wages. Capital gains: 7% on gains over 278K + 2.9% surcharge on gains over 1M = 9.9% top rate.

B&O Tax (tiered per HB 2081, Oct 2025): Retailing 0.471%, Manufacturing 0.484%, Services <1M 1.5%, Services 1-5M 1.75%, Services >5M 2.1%.

---

## Wyoming
No state income tax. No capital gains tax.
